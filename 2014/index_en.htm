The Treasure Hunt trophies

Be part of the revolution, getting your hands on one of the smartest, funnest, and most programmable watches available today.

The first three winning teams will each get an amazing Pebble smartwatch for each of their team members.

Be part of the most entertaining and high-tech Treasure Hunt on the island! Get your smart-phone and follow the clues to find the Treasure. The first three teams to collect the highest score will receive amazing trophies.

Watch with us The startup kids, a documentary about young web entrepreneurs in Europe and North America. UCLan Cyprus' state of the art auditorium, which was awarded for having the most advanced audio-visual technology in an academic institute in Europe guarantees an unforgettable experience.
