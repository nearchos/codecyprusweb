Code Cyprus 2019

Robot Adventures

For kids — ages 4 or 5 years old — and their parent



This year we invite parents to come with their kids and test out Robot Adventures, a novel robotics programme.

Robot Adventures combines playful learning with a kids-designed adventure. Through programming, kids develop critical thinking and problem solving skills.
GRASSROOTS
With Dr Panayiotis Andreou and Dr Irene Polycarpou and Dr Eliana Stavrou

Each kid must be accompanied by a parent or guardian to assist during the workshop. Each parent or guardian can accompany one or two kids.
This 45-minute workshop is independent of the remaining Code Cyprus programme. It takes place at 9:30 - 10:15 in room 018. Please arrive 15 minutes early to complete your registration.
Just like all activities in Code Cyprus, this workshop is free.
